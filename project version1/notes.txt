Instructions:

Click on the screen to add nodes.
Press C to clear the graph, R to generate a random graph.
Press B for BFS and D for DFS to see the traversal animations
You can now press E to exit the program.
If you click on a node and then another node, an edge will be created.

Press S to select a start node. Click on the desired node, and it will be highlighted in green to indicate that it is the start node
